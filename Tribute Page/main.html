virat kohli

Indian cricket player

Full Name: Virat Kohli Date of Birth: November 5, 1988 Place of Birth: Delhi, India Nationality: Indian Height: Approximately 5 feet 9 inches (175 cm) Role: Batsman Batting Style: Right-handed Bowling Style: Right-arm medium Career Highlights: Virat Kohli made his debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008 in an ODI match against Sri Lanka. He made his Test debut in June 2011 against the West Indies. Kohli is known for his aggressive batting style, consistency, and remarkable run-scoring abilities across formats. He has been one of the most successful and consistent batsmen in international cricket, breaking numerous records. Kohli has been the captain of the Indian cricket team in various formats, leading them to significant victories. Under his captaincy, India achieved their first-ever Test series victory in Australia in the 2018-2019 season. He is also known for his sportsmanship and dedication to fitness
